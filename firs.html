Date of Birth: March 9, 1951
Place of Birth: Mumbai, India
Full Name: Zakir Hussain Qureshi
Parents:
Father: Ustad Alla Rakha (legendary tabla maestro and an essential figure in Hindustani classical music)
Mother: Bavi Begum
Zakir Hussain inherited his musical talent from his father, Ustad Alla Rakha, who was a key collaborator of sitar maestro Pandit Ravi Shankar.
Growing up in a musical household, Zakir was immersed in the rich traditions of Indian classical music from a very young age.
